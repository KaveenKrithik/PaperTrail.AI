# Justification of Project SDG

## Primary SDG Alignment
**SDG 16: Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions**
- PaperTrail.AI improves transparency and accountability in business processes by producing auditable, evidence-backed verification reports.
- By detecting inconsistencies and potential fraud early, the platform strengthens institutional integrity and reduces compliance risk.
- The counter-auditor workflow provides checks and balances that support fair, explainable decision-making.

## Secondary SDG Alignment
**SDG 9: Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure**
- The project advances AI-driven infrastructure for document compliance, enabling organizations to modernize audit workflows.

**SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production**
- Automated verification reduces redundant manual processing, improves resource efficiency, and helps prevent wasteful or incorrect transactions.
